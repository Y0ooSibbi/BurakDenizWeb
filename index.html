About Me

Burak Deniz is a Turkish actor and model. He is best known for his lead role as Murat Sarsılmaz in the television series Aşk Laftan Anlamaz, and later as Barış Aktan in the television series Bizim Hikaye, and as Mehmet Kadir Bilmez in the internet series Yarım Kalan Aşklar.

Burak Deniz was learning acting skills since he was in high school, he loves acting since his childhood. In just a few years of attending acting workshops, he adopted mature acting skills. During a stage performance casting director Gökçe Doruk Erten shaw him while performing, he loved his acting in the first view later Erten helped him to start his career in the Turkish entertainment industry. In 2011 he debuts his career in the Turkish entertainment industry from a Turkish film 'Kolej Günlüğü' as a supporting role of 'Onur'. During the years 2011 to 2016 Burak Deniz did many supporting roles in various movies, he was continuously doing hard practice to improve his acting skills.It was the year 2016 when he got his big breakthrough in his career, he signed for a lead character in a Turkish television drama series 'Aşk Laftan Anlamaz' in which his character name was 'Murat Sarsılmaz'. He gained his popularity from this television series and now he is a well-established actor in the Turkish television industry. Burak Deniz has done more than 15+ television series and music videos.
